Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 16, 2025

CBA Digital (“CBA Digital,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) provides operations and digital consulting services for small businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website or otherwise interact with us.

By using this website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the site.

1. Information We Collect

Information you choose to provide

When you interact with us, you may provide information such as your name, email address, business name and role, phone number, and any other details you include in a message or form. You typically provide this information when you submit the contact form, email us, or schedule a call.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the website, basic technical information may be collected automatically, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, general location, and which pages you visit. This information is used in aggregate to understand how the site is used and to improve it.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide the services you request.
  • Communicate with you about projects, scheduling, and next steps.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website and our services.
  • Keep internal records related to current and potential clients.
  • Comply with legal obligations or protect our rights, where necessary.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Legal Bases (EEA/UK Visitors)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process your personal data based on:

  • Contract – to respond to your request or perform a contract with you.
  • Legitimate interests – to run and improve the business and communicate with you, where these interests are not outweighed by your rights.
  • Consent – where required by law; you may withdraw consent at any time.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers who help operate the website, manage communications, or store data (for example, email or hosting providers). These providers are given only the information needed to perform their work and are expected to protect it.

We may also share information if required by law, to respond to legal requests, to protect the rights and safety of CBA Digital or others, or in connection with a business transaction such as a merger or transfer of assets.

5. Data Retention

We keep personal information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including to respond to your inquiry, maintain an active working relationship, or comply with legal and tax requirements. You may ask us to delete information about you using the contact details below.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data, or to object to certain types of processing. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below. We may need to verify your identity before responding. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Third‑Party Links

This website may link to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third‑party sites, and you should review their privacy policies before providing information to them.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults working in or on behalf of a business. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Any changes will be effective when posted on this page.
