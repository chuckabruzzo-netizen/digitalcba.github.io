How it works

A practical path out of daily firefighting

This work is designed to reduce noise, not add process. We focus on reality first, then make the smallest changes that create the most relief.

1) Map what’s really happening

We trace how work enters the business, how it moves, and where it stalls — without assumptions or idealized workflows.

2) Identify the failure points

We surface where handoffs break, rework starts, and why the owner keeps getting pulled back into day-to-day decisions.

3) Set clear near-term priorities

You get a short, ranked list of what to address in the next 30–60 days — and what to intentionally ignore for now.

4) Improve execution (optional)

If you want help implementing, we keep it time-boxed, practical, and focused — no unnecessary tools, meetings, or bureaucracy.
