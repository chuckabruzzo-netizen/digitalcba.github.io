Services

I offer three ways to get unstuck: a quick Snapshot, a deeper Tune‑Up, or ongoing support.

Operations & Online Presence Snapshot

Short, focused assessment

One focused review of your in‑store operations and digital presence. Includes a 1‑day audit, simple process maps, and a 2–3 page summary with quick wins you can implement right away.

Operations Tune‑Up

Project-based pricing

A deeper engagement to map 2–3 core workflows, identify bottlenecks, and deliver a 60–90 day improvement roadmap plus a KPI starter spreadsheet tailored to your business.

Ongoing Support

Ongoing support, billed monthly

Ongoing support that combines operations advisory with light digital maintenance: check‑ins, implementation help, scheduled social posts, and simple analytics so you know what’s working.

How it works

1. Short conversation

A 20‑minute conversation to understand your business, tools, and pain points, and to decide whether a Snapshot or Tune‑Up makes sense.

2. Focused review

One day on‑site or remote review, plus follow‑up working sessions to map workflows, review data, and look at your website, Google profile, and social channels.

3. Practical next steps

A practical, plain‑language summary with prioritized actions, a simple KPI sheet, and options for light ongoing support if you want help implementing changes.
