About CBA Digital

CBA Digital is a one‑person fractional operations and digital practice based in Oregon City, Oregon. The focus is on helping local businesses— retail shops, trades, and service companies—run smoother and show up better online.

With more than 15 years in business systems analysis, telecom and dispatch operations, and hands‑on small‑business web work, CBA Digital brings a practical, systems‑first approach: no jargon, no bloated slide decks, just changes you can actually implement.

The work is grounded in simple tools, clear communication, and meeting you where you are—whether that’s a paper calendar, a POS system, or a mix of spreadsheets and sticky notes.
