About CBA Digital

CBA Digital is a one‑person operations and digital practice based in Oregon City, Oregon. The focus is on helping local businesses— retail shops, trades, and service companies—run smoother and show up better online.

With more than 15 years in business systems analysis, telecom and dispatch operations, and hands‑on small‑business web work, CBA Digital brings a practical, systems‑first approach: no jargon, no bloated slide decks, just changes you can actually implement. I’ve lived in and around Oregon City for over 30 years, and the goal is simple: helping my own neighbors and community thrive.

The work is grounded in simple tools, clear communication, and meeting you where you are—whether that’s a paper calendar, a register or checkout system, or a mix of spreadsheets and sticky notes.
